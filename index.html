About Siw Årolilja [siːv ɔːrʊˈliːja]

I live in Bergen, Norway. A beautiful west coast city.

I am a passionate UX designer with extensive experience in user-centered design and interaction design. My ability to approach design from a user perspective, combined with my experience working across different industries and product types, allows me to deliver effective and intuitive solutions that meet user needs and expectations. I have a strong understanding of the UX design process, from user research to prototyping and testing, and have a genuine interest in creating unique and user-friendly experiences.

As a person, I am versatile, and not afraid of taking on new challenges. I have a diverse education in information science, front end development, UX design, and even archaeology! However, my main background is information science, and information- and communications technology.

In my free time I like to play games, (almost 20 years in WoW now!), going to the gym, horseriding, fencing and martial arts.

As mentioned, I live in Bergen, Norway, but I'm not afraid of working remote. Where I lay my laptop is my office. I like to travel, a lot. And I love exploring new countries and cities.
